About Us

At Hydro-Track, we are redefining the way households manage their most precious resource—water. With a vision to promote water conservation and smarter living, we’ve developed a comprehensive Smart Water Monitoring System that integrates advanced IoT technology and an intuitive interface.

Our system empowers you to:

Monitor water levels in real-time, ensuring you never run out unexpectedly.
Assess water quality, helping you determine if it’s safe for drinking or other uses.
Track water consumption trends, giving you insights into your usage patterns over time.
Receive timely alerts, so you can take action before water shortages occur.
Easily book water tankers, providing a hassle-free solution to meet your needs.

Beyond functionality, we’re committed to sustainability. By enabling households to manage water more efficiently, we aim to reduce waste and contribute to global water conservation efforts.
